Welcome to TeavoU

The ultimate tea house based in Indonesia. For more than a decade serving the best tea experience for our tea lovers around the world.

Our specialty lies within the artistry of tea-making. Ensuring every process is taken with a good care, for your brew-tea-ful day!

Our Commitment

TeavoU is leading the company with the aim to ensure that future generation will be able to enjoy the same quality tea as we do today. The goal is to help tea gardens flourish like vineyards, and for our customers to appreciate tea like fine wine.

Our Vision

TeavoU passion to discover delicious, healthy beverage options by delivering high quality teas specially tailored for each of our communitea lifestyle. We want to change the way people feel about tea, one cup at a time.
